Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how our Android application collects, uses, and protects user data.

Data We Collect

Our Android application does not collect personal data from users. We do not request names, email addresses, phone numbers, or any other information that can identify users.

Anonymous Usage Data and Analytics

To improve the application and enhance user experience, we utilize Firebase Analytics, a service provided by Google, to collect anonymous usage data. This data collection is designed to help us understand how users interact with our application and identify areas for improvement.

The anonymous usage data we collect may include, but is not limited to:

  • Application usage frequency and session duration
  • Feature usage patterns and interactions with content
  • Device information (such as device type, operating system version, and screen resolution) - all anonymized
  • General geographic location data (country/region level only, not precise location)
  • App performance metrics and crash reports

All data collected through Firebase Analytics is anonymous and cannot be used to identify individual users. This information is aggregated and analyzed to help us improve app functionality, fix technical issues, and optimize the user experience. We do not combine this anonymous usage data with any personal information, and it cannot be traced back to you as an individual.

Third-Party Services

Our Android application integrates with Firebase, a service provided by Google, for analytics and app improvement purposes. Firebase Analytics processes anonymous usage data in accordance with Google's privacy policies. For more information about how Google handles data, please visit Google's Privacy Policy.

We do not share data with any other third parties. The application contains no advertisements, external links, or any other methods for third parties to collect data beyond the anonymous analytics described above.

Data Security

Although we do not collect personal data, we take all reasonable measures to ensure that all data within the application is secure and protected from unauthorized access. Firebase employs industry-standard security measures to protect the anonymous usage data it processes on our behalf.

Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to understand how your data is being used. Since we only collect anonymous usage data that cannot identify you personally, there is no personal information to access, modify, or delete. If you have concerns about anonymous analytics data collection, you may contact us using the information provided in the Contact section below.

Children's Privacy

This application is intended for children and does not collect personal data. We respect laws protecting children online, including the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA). The anonymous usage data we collect through Firebase Analytics does not include any information that can identify children, and we do not knowingly collect, store, or share any personal data from children.

Changes to the Privacy Policy

This privacy policy may be updated from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or applicable laws. All changes will be posted within the application or on our official website. We encourage you to review this policy periodically to stay informed about how we protect your privacy.

Contact

If you have any questions, concerns, or requests regarding our privacy policy or data practices, you can contact us at: support@guidingwave.com

Thank you for using our application!
